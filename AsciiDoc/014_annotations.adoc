=== Annotations, tips and callouts

You can write tips into your document with the following syntax.

----
[TIP]
====
Think of c1..c2 as _all commits as of c1 (not including c1) until commit
c2._
====
----

The output looks like the following.

[TIP]
====
Think of c1..c2 as _all commits as of c1 (not including c1) until commit
c2._
====
